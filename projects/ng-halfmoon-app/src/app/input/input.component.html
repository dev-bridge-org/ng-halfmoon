Input

Basic Usage

To apply the basic styles to your input simply add hmInput to your input-element.
This directive adds all you need to start developing your forms with input-elements.

There is also a possibility to make your input readonly by just adding the readonly attribute to your element

Sizing

There are three different sizes you can apply to your input-element.
You can apply a size by filling out the Input of the hmInput Directive called sizing.
When you want the normal size then simple leave out the Input because the default is normal.

  • large (lg)
  • normal
  • small (sm)

Disabled

Disabling your input is super easy just add disabled to your input and it should be disabled.

Validation Template-Driven

The validation is acomplished inside of the hmInput Directive.
As you might know in Angular there are two different ways of building your forms.
The first and more easy to use one is Template-Driven Forms where you define your form inside of the template.
You can try the validation by adding a required to your input-element than focus in and out of your input.
There should now be a red-border around your input.

Validation Reactive

The second is Reactive-Forms where you define your form in your typescript code in a more declarative way.
You can try the validation by adding a create a FormControl and adding Validators.required to your control than focus in and out of your input.
There should now be a red-border around your input.

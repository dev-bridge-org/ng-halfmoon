Full example

Below you can see what the hm-form-group is capable of.
You can group a label, an input, an error-text and a hint to one field
After this example we will tell you the details about the single parts of a form-group

Field is required Only alphanumeric characters and underscores allowed. Only alphanumeric characters and underscores allowed.

Connect input with label

You can add a label by adding it inside of the hm-form-group.
Currently you need to connect it to your control via an identifier and adding the for attribute to the label Once your control has the required attribute your label will get a * added to it.

Giving hints to the user

You can give the user some information about what he is supposed to enter in your field.
Simply add the hm-hint component inside your hm-form-group and enter your information.

Only alphabetic characters allowed

Giving feedback to the user

If you want to let the user know what's wrong with his input then you can do this by adding the hm-error component. Inside the component you can declare the error-text that is prompted to the user.

Only alphabetic characters allowed
